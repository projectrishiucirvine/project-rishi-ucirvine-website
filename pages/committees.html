Committees

Committees are the teams that turn ideas into action within Project RISHI. Each group focuses on a different part of the organization's mission. By working in smaller, dedicated teams, members can take ownership of projects, collaborate closely, and contribute their skills towards meaningful impact.

Youth Education

The Project RISHI Youth Education Committee develops educational, leadership, and mentorship initiatives to create sustainable opportunities for youth in underserved communities in India and locally. Our work centers on drug‑abuse awareness, self‑defense education, and promoting access to learning. We currently aim to help UCI students understand the learning gaps faced by children in rural India and the broader challenges within the youth‑education landscape.

Reproductive

In Reproductive Health, we address the biggest issues that surround women in this category by creating online workshops and pamphlets to educate everyone on basic tips that they should know. We also research the most common problems that rural women face without the proper facilities and look for ways to combat that. We do this by hosting community service events, such as donation drives to create hygiene kits with those items, online teaching workshops to discuss the basics of reproductive health with schools, and collaborating with other NGOs in Orange County, because these are issues that every woman must know of regardless of region. For us, the question isn’t “how to save the village;” it is how to serve women everywhere according to their needs to make a bigger difference to our general community. We are always open to accepting new members who are just as enthusiastic about the cause as we are!

Computer Literacy

The Project RISHI Computer Literacy Committee develops technology, digital-skills, and computer-education initiatives to create sustainable opportunities for underserved communities in India and locally. Our work centers on expanding access to digital resources, teaching foundational computer skills, and promoting technological literacy. We currently aim to help UCI students understand the digital divide faced by children and families in rural India and the broader challenges surrounding equitable access to technology.

Clinical Health

The Clinical Health Committee focuses on promoting healthcare awareness and preventive care in underserved communities. Through education-based projects, we aim to provide accessible information on common health issues, encourage healthy habits, and support long-term well-being. Our goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge and resources they need to take charge of their health and prevent avoidable illnesses.
