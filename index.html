About Project Rishi at UCI

Project RISHI is a non-profit with the goal to promote sustainable development and growth of rural Indian communities.


In partnership with local community members and NGOs in India, we identify issues central to our target community and provide resources to implement solutions through extensive field research and on-campus initiatives. UCI Project RISHI was founded in 2025.
